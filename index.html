Featured application: GammaJ
   GammaJ - a free tool for display color adjustment. It allows to change monitor gamma ramp without the need of accessing third party files (game textures / video player settings / images / etc.). Brightening a dark game which does not support advanced settings becomes very easy with just few slider drags. Dual monitors and multiple monitors supported.

You can change gamma, contrast, brightness or temperature. Invert RGB channels ramp values. Enable sRGB temperature correction. Save favorite color profiles and load them later. Setup global hotkeys for loading color settings on the fly. Acquire effect of gaming glassess just by changing color temperature.
